Purpose

Range of devices for electric dynamic braking of AC motors with maximum current 150A, maximum voltage 440V and working range from 1 to 15 sec.

Operating principle

Dropping of direct current through two of the motor windings during one half of the supply voltage and short coupling of the dips during the other half-period. The current value depends on the position of the trimmer and the motor power. the braking performance is determined by the trimmer and can be set within a range of 1 to 15 seconds depending on the type of emergency brake.

Working conditions

Може да пише нещо

Upon occurrence of an emergency event, the relay circuit must disconnect the three-phase power supply of the motor. Then connect the motor and high-voltage power supply (100-440V) to terminals L, M and LM, and to terminals A1 and A2 to supply 24V AC for power supply and synchronization with the high voltage. About 100msec. after the 24V supply, the brake starts to work. After the time specified by the trimmer has elapsed, the brake breaks the current through the motor. 100msec. later it opens the contact between terminals 11 and 12.
