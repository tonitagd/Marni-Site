About us

Located in Bulgaria

The company was established in 2000. It started its activity with the manufacture of MB-series electronic devices for emergency electrodynamic braking of asynchronous motors.

The company works with:

  • production of devices for emergency electrodynamic braking of asynchronous motors with rated power up to 3x415V and rated power up to 22kW.
  • electronic installation of new machines
  • machines retrofit
  • industrial automation
  • delivery of electronics, cables ls and switches
